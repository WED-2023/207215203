    Q&A

    Technology and Interaction:
    1. Web1: Static, read-only web pages.
    2. Web2: Interactive, read-write web with user-generated content and social media.
    3. Web3: Decentralized, read-write-trust web using blockchain for data control and transactions.
    Ownership and Control of Data:
    1. Web1: Content mostly created and controlled by website owners.
    2. Web2: Users create content, but large companies control the data.
    3. Web3: Users control and own their data through blockchain technologies.
    Economic Models:
    1. Web1: Based on advertising and direct selling.
    2. Web2: Sophisticated models including targeted advertising, e-commerce, and subscriptions.
    3. Web3: Token-based economies and microtransactions enabled by cryptocurrencies and smart contracts.
